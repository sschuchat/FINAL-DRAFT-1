The Problem

Your community is busy. You just can't find it.

Local events are scattered across Facebook groups, flyers on coffee shop corkboards, Eventbrite links buried in email, and city websites that haven't been updated since 2014. The result? Residents miss what's happening in their own neighborhood — and local governments lose one of their best tools for civic engagement.

🔍

Fragmented information

The average resident checks 4+ platforms to figure out what's happening this weekend. Most give up before they find it.

📉

Low civic turnout

Town halls, council meetings, and public forums go under-attended — not because people don't care, but because they never knew.

🏛️

Cities have no central channel

Municipalities rely on outdated websites and press releases to reach residents who live on their phones.

The Solution

One app. Every event. Powered by your city.

CivicCircle partners directly with local municipalities to centralize every public and private event happening in town — then puts it in the pocket of every resident. Free for community members, subscription-based for cities, and designed from the ground up to make showing up easy.

  • Unified calendar for civic, cultural, and commercial events
  • Built-in ticketing for concerts, sports, and paid experiences
  • Push notifications, SMS, and email for events you care about
  • Municipal dashboard for city staff to publish in seconds

Features that Actually Matter

Every feature solves a real problem.

01

Unified Event Feed

So you stop checking five apps.

A single scrollable feed surfaces every event in your city, filtered by interest, distance, and time.

02

Municipal Dashboard

So city staff reach residents in minutes, not weeks.

Publish, edit, and broadcast civic events from one clean interface — no web developer required.

03

Smart Notifications

So you never miss what you'd actually attend.

Opt in to categories and neighborhoods — get pushed only what matches.

04

In-App Ticketing

So buying tickets doesn't mean five redirects.

Purchase tickets for paid events without leaving CivicCircle. Proceeds flow back into the local economy.

05

Civic Engagement Layer

So democracy stops being homework.

Town halls, public comment windows, and local elections show up alongside concerts — not buried in PDFs.

06

Save, Share, Show Up

So your plans become plans with people.

Save events, share with friends, sync to calendar, and RSVP — all in two taps.
