Marketing Strategy

We Don't Pull People In. We Meet Them Where They Already Are.

CivicCircle's growth strategy is inbound by design. Following the frameworks developed by HubSpot and detailed in David Meerman Scott's The New Rules of Marketing and PR, we create content and experiences valuable enough that our customers find us — not the other way around.

01
Attract

Useful, civic-minded content that shows up when people search for what's happening locally.

02
Engage

Conversations that treat residents and city officials as neighbors, not leads.

03
Delight

An experience worth telling a friend — our most important acquisition channel.
